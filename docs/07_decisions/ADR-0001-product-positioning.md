# ADR-0001: Product Positioning

## Status

Accepted

## Decision

D3HR is a Korean public-institution HR Evidence OS and Job Intelligence Layer, not a generic HR SaaS and not an ERP replacement first.

## Context

Public institutions need job-centered HR, performance evidence, career development, workload visibility, NCS usage, auditability, and management-evaluation evidence.

Legacy ERP stores records but does not create job-centered HR decision evidence.

## Consequences

- Product modules center on JobDB, evidence, career, policy, and auditability.
- ERP integration is a strategy, not the initial product identity.
- NCS-to-JobDB is a core hook.
